
	Architectural entrance accents (Phase 4)

	Vendored from two Kenney kits (both CC0):

	  Furniture Kit       — doorArch (doorwayFront), pottedPlant
	                        (self-contained: UniGLTF, KHR_materials_unlit +
	                        baseColorFactor — no external texture atlas)
	  City Kit (Suburban) — railing (fence), planter
	                        (UnityGLTF export — these reference a shared
	                        Textures/colormap.png by relative URI, vendored
	                        alongside at architecture/Textures/colormap.png)
